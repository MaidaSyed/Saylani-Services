saylani services

Food

Considering Khidmat-e-Khalaq as an act of worship, more than 625 Dastarkhuwan are set up in all the branches and different places of Saylani Welfare International Trust where around 300,000 people are treated with food supply every day at all three times of the day with respect. These Dastarkhuwan welcome everyone, regardless of ethnicity or color, and offer a very fresh meal consisting of vegetables, pulses, and goat meat in a clean environment.Adopting the great spirit of welfare society, the Saylani Trust, in collaboration with Fixit and Haier, has made available the facility of Host Saylani Refrigerators outside its offices. People in need can get food at any time without any hindrance for themselves and their family. While a large number of people keep food and drink in these refrigerators.Saylani Welfare used to provide only two meals a day and also provide breakfast for patients in schools and hospitals in poor communities but now Saylani Welfare International Trust is also offering breakfast with healthy ingredients on all its tablecloths. Which is presented at the banquet of those who go to work in the morning.

Education

Today is the age of information technology and we aim to equip our youth with computer programming skills and prepare them for the development of the country. In this regard, our Saylani Mass IT Training Program providing vocational training to more than 75,000 students in Web and Mobile Application Development, CCNA, Graphics Designing, Computer Based Accounting in Karachi, Hyderabad, Faisalabad, Islamabad. Our goal is to develop more than 1 million software developers. across the country, which will add about $100 billion annually to Pakistan's economy.Saylani Welfare has been providing primary and secondary education to millions of children. Under this, many children from poor families are becoming an asset to the country through education. Now Saylani has set up a regular academy named Saylani Al- Mohsin Academy has been set up. Under this academy, free educational facilities will be provided to students in accordance with international standards.Saylani Welfare is the only organization in Pakistan that has been providing education from Montessori to CA. To this end, the organization has set up SBIL (Visiting Schools of Business and Islamic Leadership) where ACCA, FIA, CA and other professional degrees are also being taught. This project is approved by The Institute of Chartered Accountants.

Medical

The Saylani Medical and Diagnostic Center provides quality healthcare facilities in which General O.P.D, Consultant Clinic, Diabetes, Hepatitis Treatment Center, Ophthalmology and O.P.D. It also provides dental services, laboratory tests, physiotherapy, mobile health clinics and oriental clinic facilities including cupping therapy, homeopathy and wisdom.Thalassemia is an incurable disease. Saylani Welfare has taken the initiative to eradicate Thalassemia from Pakistan. For this purpose, Saylani Welfare has also set up Blood Bank and Thalassemia Care Center where hundreds of children are being transfused every month. All these facilities are provided for the sake of Allah.Saylani healthcare services provide free medical supplies to help patients in need. These include oxygen cylinders, nebulizers, hospital beds, stretchers, wheelchairs and walking stacks. They provide goods to people on a temporary or future basis. Low-income earners can avail this facility on a refundable nominal deposit.

About Saylani

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-government organization (NGO) focusing primarily on feeding the poor and homeless. It was established in May 1999 and is headquartered at Bahdurabad, Karachi, Pakistan. It was founded and headed by spiritual and religious scholar Maulana Bashir Farooq Qadri.
